Oracle Reserve (Dynamic Supply)

In addition to the fixed distribution, the Oracle manages a virtual reserve of up to 200M tokens that can be minted or burned based on news sentiment:

  • BURN Events: When sentiment is negative (scandals, crises), tokens are sent to dead address, permanently reducing supply.
  • MINT Events: When sentiment is positive, new tokens are released into the Liquidity Pool.
  • Reserve Limits: Min 50M, Max 400M ‚Äî prevents extreme supply changes.
  • Cooldown: 60 seconds minimum between Oracle actions.
