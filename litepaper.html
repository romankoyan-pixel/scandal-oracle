Oracle Reserve Mechanics

The Oracle Reserve is the heart of SCANDAL's dynamic tokenomics. This 200M token reserve is managed exclusively by the Oracle smart contract:

  • BURN Events: Tokens are sent from Oracle Reserve to the dead address (0x000...dEaD), permanently removing them from circulation.
  • MINT Events: Tokens are released from Oracle Reserve back into the Liquidity Pool, increasing circulating supply.
  • Reserve Limits: Minimum 50M tokens must remain in reserve to prevent excessive burning. Maximum 400M cap prevents over-minting.
